# Thurr Solutions Diagnostic Sales Version

## Purpose

The Thurr Solutions Diagnostic is a client-facing lead-generation and sales asset that turns a prospect's current business situation into a clear growth systems roadmap.

It should feel less like a generic audit and more like a preview of what Thurr Solutions can build:

> See where your growth system is leaking, what to fix first, and what Thurr can build to turn more attention into booked revenue.

The diagnostic creates trust before the consult. It gives the prospect useful clarity, frames Thurr Solutions as the execution partner, and makes the paid buildout feel like the natural next step.

## Offer Positioning

### Core Offer

Use:

> Get your free Thurr Solutions Growth Systems Diagnostic.

Stronger:

> See what's blocking your leads, sales, follow-up, and automation in one custom growth systems report.

Most direct:

> Find the gaps in your sales system before they cost you another month of revenue.

### What The Prospect Gets

The diagnostic gives the prospect:

- A plain-English review of their current growth system
- A score across the major parts of the customer journey
- The highest-impact bottlenecks to fix first
- Recommended Thurr Solutions build blocks
- A practical next step for turning the findings into a working system

### Positioning Guardrails

Avoid making the offer sound like a shallow website audit.

Avoid:

> Get a free funnel audit.

Better:

> Get a free growth systems diagnostic.

Best:

> Get a custom map of the systems your business needs to attract, convert, and follow up with better leads.

Use this as the opening copy for the generated diagnostic report:

> Your Thurr Solutions Growth Systems Diagnostic reviews the key parts of your current customer journey: visibility, offer clarity, conversion path, follow-up, automation, and revenue readiness.
>
> The goal is simple: show where your current system is helping you grow, where it is leaking opportunity, and what should be built next.

## Score Framing

The score should make the report easy to understand without making the prospect feel judged.

Use:

> Your Growth System Score reflects how ready your current setup is to consistently attract, convert, and follow up with qualified leads.

Score bands:

- 0-39: Early Stage System
- 40-59: Leaky Growth System
- 60-79: Solid Foundation
- 80-100: Scalable Growth System

### Score Category Copy

#### Early Stage System

> Your business has opportunity, but the system around that opportunity is still underbuilt. The biggest wins will come from clarifying the offer, creating a stronger conversion path, and putting basic follow-up in place.

#### Leaky Growth System

> You have pieces of a growth system, but they are not working together cleanly yet. Leads may be coming in inconsistently, follow-up may be manual, and prospects may not have a clear next step.

#### Solid Foundation

> Your business has a workable foundation. The next level is tightening the customer journey, improving conversion points, and using automation to remove manual drag from your sales process.

#### Scalable Growth System

> Your business is close to having a system that can scale. The priority is optimization: stronger data, better automation, cleaner handoffs, and more predictable revenue workflows.

## Diagnostic Categories

### Visibility

Measures whether the business is easy for the right people to find and understand.

Sales framing:

> If the right people cannot quickly see what you do, who you help, and why it matters, the rest of the system has to work harder than it should.

### Offer Clarity

Measures whether the offer is specific, compelling, and easy to act on.

Sales framing:

> Clear offers convert faster because they reduce the amount of thinking a prospect has to do before taking the next step.

### Conversion Path

Measures whether the website, landing page, form, booking flow, or sales path gives prospects a simple way to move forward.

Sales framing:

> Traffic is only valuable when there is a clear path from interest to action.

### Follow-Up

Measures whether leads are contacted, nurtured, and re-engaged consistently.

Sales framing:

> Most businesses do not only need more leads. They need a better system for handling the leads they already get.

### Automation

Measures whether repetitive sales, onboarding, reporting, and customer communication tasks are handled by systems.

Sales framing:

> Automation should not replace the relationship. It should protect the relationship by making sure important moments are not missed.

### Revenue Readiness

Measures whether the business can track pipeline, bookings, customer value, and next actions.

Sales framing:

> A growth system should make revenue easier to see, forecast, and improve.

## Recommended Solution Blocks

Use these blocks inside the report to connect diagnostic findings to paid Thurr Solutions services.

### Growth System Foundation

Best for prospects with low clarity, scattered assets, or no clean customer journey.

Includes:

- Offer and audience clarification
- Landing page or core website path
- Lead capture form
- CRM setup
- Basic follow-up sequence
- Reporting snapshot

Sales copy:

> The first priority is building the foundation: a clear offer, a clean path to convert interest into leads, and a simple backend system that keeps every opportunity visible.

### Lead Capture And Conversion Buildout

Best for prospects who have attention but weak conversion.

Includes:

- Landing page strategy
- CTA rewrite
- Form or booking flow
- Trust-building page sections
- Confirmation and thank-you experience
- Conversion tracking

Sales copy:

> Your current system can do more with the traffic and attention it already has. This buildout focuses on turning more visitors, referrals, and interested prospects into real conversations.

### Follow-Up Automation System

Best for prospects losing leads after the first touch.

Includes:

- Email and SMS follow-up flows
- Missed lead reminders
- CRM pipeline stages
- Sales task alerts
- Re-engagement sequence
- Internal notification workflow

Sales copy:

> The money is often in the follow-up. This system makes sure new leads are acknowledged quickly, routed correctly, and followed up with until they book, buy, or clearly opt out.

### Sales Operations Dashboard

Best for prospects who need visibility into pipeline, activity, and revenue.

Includes:

- Lead source tracking
- Pipeline dashboard
- Revenue snapshot
- Task and follow-up visibility
- Weekly owner report
- Automation health checks

Sales copy:

> This gives the business owner a clear command center for leads, sales activity, and revenue movement, so growth is not hidden inside inboxes, spreadsheets, and memory.

### AI-Assisted Business System

Best for prospects ready to use AI inside their sales, support, content, or operations workflow.

Includes:

- AI intake or qualification assistant
- AI-generated lead summaries
- AI follow-up drafts
- AI reporting summaries
- Internal knowledge workflow
- Human approval points

Sales copy:

> AI is most useful when it is connected to a real business process. This block adds AI where it can save time, improve speed, and support better decisions without removing human control.

### Full Growth Systems Buildout

Best for prospects with strong opportunity and multiple disconnected systems.

Includes:

- Strategy and offer refinement
- Landing page or website conversion path
- CRM and pipeline setup
- Follow-up automation
- Reporting dashboard
- AI workflow opportunities
- Launch support

Sales copy:

> This is the complete Thurr Solutions buildout: the visible sales path, the backend workflows, the follow-up engine, and the reporting layer working together as one growth system.

## Handoff From Diagnostic To Paid Buildout

The report should guide the prospect through this sequence:

1. Understand the current state
2. See the score and strongest bottlenecks
3. Recognize the cost of leaving the gaps unfixed
4. Review the recommended Thurr Solutions build blocks
5. Book a consult to choose the first paid buildout
6. Approve scope, timeline, and investment
7. Move into strategy, implementation, launch, and optimization

## Internal Sales Notes

Use the diagnostic as a sales asset, not only a report.

For each lead, identify:

- Primary score band
- Top bottleneck
- Best recommended solution block
- Urgency level
- Likely budget fit
- Consult CTA status
- Proposed first buildout

Sales rep question set:

- Which finding felt most accurate?
- Where are leads or sales currently slipping through?
- What are you still handling manually?
- If this system worked better in 30 days, what would change?
- Do you want the first buildout to focus on leads, follow-up, automation, or reporting?

Close with:

> The report already shows the build priorities. The consult is where we choose the first system and map the implementation.
