## Blunt Scope Critique

Do not jump straight to a full custom app.

Restore first needs a clear lead-to-close workflow:

1. Lead comes in.
2. Team qualifies the job.
3. Inspection is scheduled.
4. Status is tracked.
5. Estimate/proposal follow-up happens.
6. Closed/lost outcome is logged.

Once that workflow is proven, then a custom dashboard or portal can make sense.
