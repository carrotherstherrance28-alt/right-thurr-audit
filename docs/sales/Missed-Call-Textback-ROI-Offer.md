# Missed-Call Text-Back + ROI Calculator Offer

## Positioning

Most local service businesses do not lose every lead because they need more ads. They lose warm opportunities because somebody called, nobody answered, and there was no fast follow-up path.

This offer gives the business a simple recovery system:

- Missed call happens
- Automatic text goes out
- Lead can reply by SMS
- Owner/team gets an alert
- Lead is logged
- Follow-up task is created
- ROI calculator estimates what recovered calls may be worth

## Buyer

Best fit:

- Roofing and contractors
- HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and handyman services
- Beauty, med spa, and appointment-based businesses
- Real estate teams
- Insurance offices

Use extra compliance review for healthcare, insurance, finance, or sensitive-data workflows.

## Simple ROI Formula

Use conservative assumptions:

```txt
Monthly missed-call value =
missed calls per month
× reply/recovery rate
× booking rate
× average job value
```

Example:

```txt
40 missed calls
× 30% recovered by text
× 40% booked
× $500 average job
= $2,400 estimated monthly recovered opportunity
```

Do not present this as a guarantee. Present it as a planning estimate.

## Compliance And Risk Notes

- SMS requires consent and opt-out awareness.
- Do not send sensitive health, insurance, or financial details by default.
- For healthcare/hospice, HIPAA review is required before any patient-related messaging.
- For insurance/finance, avoid advice claims and keep messages operational.
- Do not guarantee recovered revenue.
