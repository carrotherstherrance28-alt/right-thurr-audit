# Marketplace Launch Kit: Upwork And Fiverr

This is the launch kit for taking Thurr Solutions work to Upwork and Fiverr.

The goal is not to look like a generic AI automation freelancer. The goal is to look like an operator who can diagnose a business leak and build the first working system.

## Positioning

Primary positioning:

```txt
I build AI automation systems for local service businesses that need better lead intake, follow-up, reporting, and owner visibility.
```

Shorter version:

```txt
Private AI systems for lead intake, follow-up, and business operations.
```

Operating promise:

```txt
We build. You profit.
```

Use that as a brand promise, not as a guaranteed financial claim. Pair it with practical wording:

```txt
I build the intake, follow-up, reporting, and automation system so owners stop losing opportunities in the gaps.
```

## First Offer Stack

Start with three offers. Do not launch with ten.

### Offer 1: Lead-Flow Diagnostic

Best for Upwork proposals and Fiverr entry gig.

```txt
I will audit your lead intake and follow-up system and give you a clear automation plan.
```

Deliverables:

- Review of current website, form, booking path, or lead flow
- Scorecard for offer clarity, intake quality, response speed, follow-up, and tracking
- Biggest leak and fastest win
- Recommended first automation
- 14-day implementation path

Starting price:

```txt
$75-$150 for first marketplace traction
```

Upsell:

```txt
Apply the plan by building the first n8n/Supabase/CRM workflow.
```

### Offer 2: n8n Lead Intake Automation

Best for clients who already know they need a workflow.

```txt
I will build an n8n lead intake automation with alerts, CRM logging, and follow-up tasks.
```

Deliverables:

- Lead capture webhook or form connection
- CRM/Supabase/Google Sheet/Airtable record
- Discord, Slack, or email owner alert
- Lead status and tags
- Basic follow-up task or email draft
- Test submission and handoff notes

Starting price:

```txt
$250-$500 depending on integrations
```

### Offer 3: AI Business Buildout Blueprint

Best for Thurr-owned site leads and higher-value Upwork conversations.

```txt
I will turn your business idea into an AI business buildout plan.
```

Deliverables:

- Business opportunity summary
- Best first business model or offer
- Funnel strategy
- Automation stack
- AI agents needed
- 30-day launch roadmap
- Recommended next build

Starting price:

```txt
$150-$300 as a paid blueprint, or free as a lead magnet on the owned site.
```

## What Not To Sell First

Avoid starting with:

- giant SaaS builds
- full custom AI agents with unclear scope
- guaranteed revenue claims
- medical/legal/financial compliance-heavy automation
- broad "I can do anything with AI" gigs

Start narrow. Win trust. Expand after proof.
