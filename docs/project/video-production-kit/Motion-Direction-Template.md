Use this to turn intake into actionable shot-by-shot instructions before generating clips.

## 1) Creative Direction (Translator)

### Brand Tone

- practical

- clinical

- high-trust/professional

- What should it feel like in 1 second?

### Pacing Pattern

Choose one:

- fast hook → proof → offer → CTA
- before/after → process → proof → CTA
- product reveal → benefits → proof → CTA
- service transformation → proof → offer → CTA
- calm explainer → steps → CTA

### Camera / Motion Logic

Rules (default):

- One camera move per shot.
- One clear subject per shot.
- Prefer grounded motion (push-in, slow pan, gentle orbit).
- Avoid chaotic “everything moving” motion.

Allowed camera moves:

- locked camera
- slow push / dolly in
- slow pull back
- gentle pan (left/right)
- gentle tilt (up/down)
- slow orbit (small radius)
- close-up insert push

### Quality Boundaries (Must Avoid)

- morphing faces/hands/logos
- unreadable text overlays
- sci-fi AI visuals unless explicitly desired
- generic “luxury” stock vibes that don’t match the brand
- over-smoothing / plastic skin / fake HDR
