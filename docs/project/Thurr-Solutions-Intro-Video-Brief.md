## Recommended Placement

Put the video after the homepage hero and before the audit form.

Current website headline:

> See how a warm lead becomes a trackable next step.

Reason:

The public site should sell the buyer outcome, not the production method. NotebookLM and Runway stay in the internal workflow. The buyer-facing message is the lead path: inquiry, intake, owner alert, follow-up, and managed support.

## Buyer-Facing Message

The video should explain:

1. Leads come in from a website, booking page, form, DMs, calls, or referrals.
2. Most businesses lose money because the response path is unclear or too slow.
3. Thurr Solutions starts with a Lead Flow Audit to find the biggest leak.
4. If the website is the leak, build Growth Website + Intake.
5. If follow-up is the leak, build Automation.
6. If the system becomes important to revenue, manage it monthly.

Core line:

> The audit tells us what to fix. The build fixes it. The retainer keeps it working.

## NotebookLM Use

Use NotebookLM to turn the offer architecture, site copy, client notes, and real examples into:

- A clear narrated explainer
- A mini podcast/audio walkthrough
- Short talking points for social clips
- Slide outline for a simple visual presentation

Do not say "NotebookLM" in the main buyer-facing video unless the video is for Right Thurr/build-in-public content. For Thurr Solutions, the buyer cares about the lead system, not the tool.

## Voice Requirement

The final website intro should use Thurr's voice, not a generic AI narrator.

Voice production rules:

- Use only Thurr's own voice with his explicit approval.
- Do not upload voice samples until Thurr confirms the platform and use case.
- Keep the voiceover professional, direct, and calm.
- Avoid exaggerated influencer energy, fake urgency, or overproduced radio-announcer tone.
- Save any approved raw voice sample locally in a private, gitignored folder, not inside `public/`.

## Draft Script

Most local businesses do not only need more leads. They need a cleaner path for the leads they already get.

A new inquiry comes in from your website, booking page, Instagram, referral, or contact form. Then the clock starts. If response is slow, the details are scattered, or follow-up depends on memory, warm leads go cold.

Thurr Solutions starts with a Lead Flow Audit. We review your website, booking path, intake form, follow-up, and owner visibility. Then we identify the biggest leak closest to revenue.

If the website is the issue, we build the Growth Website and Intake path. If follow-up is the issue, we build the automation. If the system becomes business-critical, we manage it monthly.

The audit tells us what to fix. The build fixes it. The retainer keeps it working.

Start with the Lead System Audit.
