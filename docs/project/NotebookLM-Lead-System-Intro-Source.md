# Thurr Solutions Lead System Intro Source

Use this as a source in NotebookLM to generate a concise audio overview, mini podcast, and talking points for the website intro video.

## Business Context

Thurr Solutions builds and manages AI lead generation and automation systems for local service businesses. The business helps owners stop losing warm leads because of unclear websites, slow follow-up, scattered DMs, manual reminders, and missing owner visibility.

The current target markets are roofing and contracting businesses, beauty and appointment-based businesses, and insurance professionals. Healthcare and hospice work is possible but should be treated as a special-case project because HIPAA and sensitive data requirements add risk and complexity.

## Core Offer

The starting offer is the Lead Flow Audit. It is a diagnostic and first step, not the whole system.

The audit reviews:

- Website and service page clarity
- Booking or inquiry path
- Lead intake form
- Follow-up timing and consistency
- Owner visibility
- CRM, spreadsheet, or tracking setup
- Biggest revenue leak

The audit deliverable is a scorecard, priority fix list, and recommended battle plan.

## Offer Ladder

Starter: Lead Flow Audit, $250-$350.

Phase 1: Growth Website + Intake, $500-$1,500. This improves the service site, lead form, booking or inquiry flow, SEO basics, and first follow-up handoff.

Phase 2: Automation Build, $1,000-$3,500+. This connects forms, CRM or sheets, alerts, follow-up sequences, AI summaries, and routing so leads move without constant manual chasing.

Retainer: Managed AI Automation, $250-$750/month. This monitors workflows, fixes breaks, improves prompts, updates routing, reviews errors, and keeps the lead path useful after launch.

Core positioning line:

The audit tells us what to fix. The build fixes it. The retainer keeps it working.

## Buyer Problem

Most local businesses do not only need more leads. They need a cleaner path for the leads they already get.

A new inquiry can come from a website, booking page, Instagram DM, referral, form, call, or email. Once the inquiry arrives, the clock starts. If the response is slow, the details are scattered, or follow-up depends on memory, warm leads go cold.

Thurr Solutions helps by making the lead path visible, trackable, and easier to act on.

## Website Intro Video Message

The intro video should explain the process in 45-60 seconds. It should sound confident, practical, and professional. It should not sound like a course seller or generic AI hype.

The buyer should understand:

1. Where leads come from.
2. Why slow follow-up causes lost revenue.
3. Why Thurr Solutions starts with an audit.
4. How the audit naturally leads into website/intake work, automation, or managed support.
5. Why the system is built around business outcomes instead of tools.
