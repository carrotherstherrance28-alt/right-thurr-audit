# Right Thurr Brand System

## Brand Architecture

Right Thurr and Thurr Solutions should be related, but not identical.

Right Thurr is the main public-facing brand. It should feel bold, active, entrepreneurial, and memorable.

Thurr Solutions is the B2B version. It should feel more strategic, polished, reliable, and service-oriented.

The two brands should share enough DNA that people understand they belong together, but they should be clearly separated by usage, tone, and visual treatment.

## Recommended Structure

Use a master brand plus sub-brand system:

```text
Right Thurr
Consumer / creator / local entrepreneur brand

Thurr Solutions
B2B services, consulting, automation, AI systems, and business infrastructure
```

Right Thurr should be the more expressive brand.

Thurr Solutions should be the more controlled brand.

## Combined Name Guidance

Avoid making `Right Thurr Solutions` the main brand name unless the strategy intentionally changes.

The combined name can be useful as an exploratory concept, but it creates a naming problem:

- Right Thurr starts to sound like a service agency instead of a product.
- Thurr Solutions loses its cleaner B2B role.
- The customer may not understand whether they are buying an app, a service, merch, or consulting.

Recommended rule:

- Use `Right Thurr` for the product, app, merch, stickers, luggage tags, and public entrepreneur-facing brand.
- Use `Thurr Solutions` for B2B services, proposals, client work, automation consulting, and implementation.
- Use `Right Thurr by Thurr Solutions` only when you need to connect the product to the company behind it.

Good bridge lockups:

```text
Right Thurr
by Thurr Solutions
```

```text
Thurr Solutions
Builder of Right Thurr
```

```text
Right Thurr
A Thurr Solutions product
```

Use combined `Right Thurr Solutions` concept boards as visual references only unless the naming system is intentionally consolidated.

## Secondary Logo Watermark System

Use the R+T secondary logo as the official watermark across materials created by Right Thurr and Thurr Solutions.

The watermark should feel like a quiet signature, not a competing logo.

Best uses:

- Generated business blueprints
- PDF exports
- Client strategy documents
- Slide decks
- Website mockups
- Social graphics
- Product screenshots
- Luggage tag backs
- Sticker sheet backgrounds
- Proposal covers
- Automation maps

### Watermark Placement

Recommended default:

- Bottom-right corner
- 24 px to 40 px from the edge on digital assets
- 0.25 in to 0.5 in from the edge on print assets

Alternative placements:

- Center background for cover pages
- Top-right corner for internal docs
- Repeating pattern for merch, packaging, and sticker sheets

Avoid:

- Placing the watermark over important text
- Placing the watermark over faces, product images, or detailed screenshots
- Using multiple watermark styles in the same document

### Watermark Opacity

Use low opacity for normal content:

- Light background: black R+T at 6% to 12% opacity
- Dark background: white R+T at 8% to 16% opacity
- Orange background: black or white R+T at 10% to 18% opacity

Use stronger opacity only on covers, merch, or decorative layouts:

- Cover pages: 12% to 22%
- Merch patterns: 15% to 35%
- Luggage tags: full opacity when used as the main mark

### Watermark Versions Needed

Create these watermark files:

- R+T black transparent PNG
- R+T white transparent PNG
- R+T orange transparent PNG
- R+T outline-only transparent PNG
- R+T SVG master file
- R+T low-opacity document watermark PNG

### Brand Rule

The primary Right Thurr logo is the loud mark.

The R+T secondary logo is the signature mark.

Use the primary logo when the brand needs to be recognized immediately. Use the R+T watermark when the work needs to feel owned without interrupting the content.

## How To Distinguish The Two Brands

Right Thurr:

- Uses the expressive western logotype.
- Can use thicker outlines and sticker-style borders.
- Can use orange more boldly.
- Can use hand-drawn details.
- Can feel like motion, merch, travel, and entrepreneurial energy.

Thurr Solutions:

- Uses cleaner typography.
- Can borrow the R+T monogram, but should simplify the treatment.
- Should use more black, off-white, and restrained orange.
- Should avoid looking too playful.
- Should feel like the business infrastructure arm behind the brand.

## Color System

Current visible palette:

- Black
- White
- Warm orange
- Light gray/off-white background

Recommended working palette:

```text
Right Thurr Orange: #E95F24
Deep Black: #111111
Paper White: #F4F3EF
Utility Gray: #C8C6BE
Signal Green: #20A66A
Alert Red: #D83A2E
Money Gold: #D9A441
```

Use orange as the brand energy color.

Use black for authority and clarity.

Use off-white for warmth, especially in brand pages and merch mockups.

Use green, red, and gold only inside the product UI for system status, issues, and money signals.

## Gradient Recommendation

Use gradient sparingly.

The brand should not become a generic AI gradient brand. A single premium gradient version can be useful for website hero graphics, app launch moments, and limited merch.

Recommended gradient:

```text
Burnt Orange -> Hot Orange -> Gold
#C9431C -> #F06423 -> #D9A441
```

Good uses:

- One logo sheet variant
- Hero background accent
- AI Engine status glow
- Premium sticker drop
- Luggage tag limited version

Avoid:

- Putting gradients everywhere
- Purple/blue AI gradients
- Making the core logo depend on gradient to work

## Typography Direction

Right Thurr:

- Keep the western hand-drawn logo font for the logo.
- Use a clean sans-serif for UI and website body text.
- Use bold condensed headings only in limited places.

Thurr Solutions:

- Use cleaner sans-serif typography.
- Avoid heavy western display type as the main B2B wordmark.
- Keep the connection through color, icon shape, and monogram.

Recommended typography mix:

- Logo: custom western mark
- Product UI: clean sans-serif
- Headings: bold, compact sans-serif
- Documents: professional sans-serif with clear hierarchy

## Recommendation

Keep Right Thurr as the bold main brand and use Thurr Solutions as the cleaner B2B arm.

Do not combine them into one logo system that looks the same everywhere. They should feel related, but they need different jobs:

- Right Thurr gets attention.
- Thurr Solutions earns trust.

The shared bridge should be the R+T monogram, orange/black/off-white palette, bold border language, and directional arrow energy.
